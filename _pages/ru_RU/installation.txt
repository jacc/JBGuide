Have your Apple ID email and password on hand, so you can get ready to enter it when needed.
{: .notice--info}

## Installation

Once Cydia Impactor is open, it should look something like the image below.

![]({{ "/images/screenshots/impactor-view.png" | absolute_url }})
{: .notice--info}

Plug your device in. Cydia Impactor should recognize the device.

![]({{ "/images/screenshots/impactor-recognized.png" | absolute_url }})
{: .notice--info}

Drag the .IPA file that you downloaded into Cydia Impactor. If done correctly, it should ask for your Apple ID email, then password.

## Post Installation

Unlock your iDevice. If the installation completed, the Electra icon should be shown on your device homescreen.

![]({{ "/images/screenshots/electra-homescreen.jpg" | absolute_url }})
{: .notice--info}

You are now ready to install Electra!
