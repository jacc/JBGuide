## Must Install tweaks

These are the tweaks that you want to install as soon as you have jailbroken.

Use our [Prepare Tweaks](prepare-tweaks) and [Installing Tweaks](install-tweaks) pages to install these tweaks.
{: .notice--primary}

J9ck, and the editors of this guide whole-heartedly discourage piracy of tweaks.
{: .notice--warning}

# PullToRespring

[Download](http://idevicehacked.com/assets/deb/pulltorespring.deb)

PullToRespring allows quick and easy resprings. All you have to do to respring is go into your settings and pull down. This will respring your device easily and quickly.

# SystemInfo

[Download](http://apt.thebigboss.org/repofiles/cydia/debs2.0/systeminfo_1.2.1.deb)

SystemInfo adds detailed system info to your device's About page. It adds your ECID (along with the option to save blobs), UDID, Platform, Boot-nonce (and the ability to edit it), and much more.

# TapTapFlip

[Download](https://repo.cpdigitaldarkroom.com/debs_public//com.cpdigitaldarkroom.taptapflip_1.3.3_iphoneos-arm.deb)

TapTapFlip adds a very basic feature to any application: To switch the camera from front to back, or vice versa, just tap twice on the screen to change it.
