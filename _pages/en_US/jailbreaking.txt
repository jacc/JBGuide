## Inside Electra

![]({{ "/images/screenshots/electra-inside.jpeg" | absolute_url }})
{: .notice--info}

Once you have opened up Electra, it should look something like this.

Make sure that Tweaks is turned **on**. This will be important later.
{: .notice--warning}

# Jailbreaking

Click the button that says "Jailbreak". It should say:

+ Please Wait (1/3)
+ Please Wait (2/3)

After completing 2/3, the device should go black, then a spinning wheel should appear. The device is now respringing.

# Post Jailbreaking

Once your device asks for your passcode, you are now jailbroken! Congrats! The rest of this guide will be how to harness the power of your new found jailbreak.

If your device restarts, you have to re-run Electra.
{: .notice--warning}
